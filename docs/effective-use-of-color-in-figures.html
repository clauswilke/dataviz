2.4 Dos and Don’ts of color use

Figure 2.6: Population growth from 2000 to 2010 versus population size in 2000, for all 50 US states and the Discrict of Columbia. Every state is marked in a different color, but it is not possible to reliably match the colors in the legend to the dots in the scatter plot.

This figure violates two principles: (i) don’t use spurious colors; (ii) don’t use overly saturated colors.
